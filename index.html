This group is a humble freeskate/freestyle/rollerdance collaborative, and free of charge, skate accademy. We meet every Friday 8pm at The Big Fish to go on a skate route if the weather allows it. We are passionate about urban freerides, slalom and dancing on our skates. Our rounds are adaptable to beginner skaters and we adjust the level to the abilities of the participants on the go. We enjoy making new friends. Join us!
